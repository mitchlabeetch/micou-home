## Welcome Section

**Title**: `Welcome to micou.org`

**Paragraph 1**:
```
This platform hosts services I deploy, as well as tools I develop myself. Its goal is to provide free access to useful tools for social, artistic, environmental, activist, and many other activities.
```

**Paragraph 2**:
```
Here you will find secure, decentralized, and encrypted alternatives to messaging tools like Slack, private alternatives to Microsoft Office for productivity, to WeTransfer for file sharing, open knowledge directories, and other tools to support your projects.
```

**Commitments**:
1. `I have no access to your data on any of the tools you use.`
2. `No Cloud AI is natively present on the services, in order to limit environmental impact.`
3. `All services will always remain completely free.`

**Note**: `Other principles will follow; content will be progressively enriched.`

**Support**:
```
Support this project: If you would like to contribute, you can click here. In all seriousness: if you truly wish to contribute to the funding of this infrastructure, don't hesitate to write to me at micou@micou.org. I will never ask for contributions and have not set up a donation platform, but if you can and wish to, any help will be appreciated. Thank you!
```

## Terms of Use

**Title**: `Terms of Use`

**Rule 1**: `Using these services, especially when exchanging files, images, videos, or streaming, consumes resources. It must therefore be done responsibly to limit digital footprint.`

**Rule 2**: `It is strictly forbidden to use these services for commercial purposes, or for any activity promoting or enabling mass surveillance, policing, or any project whose principles or actions encourage racism, LGBTQIA+phobia, ableism, body-shaming, or any other form of discrimination.`

**Rule 3**: `Your data on each service belongs exclusively to you: it can be completely deleted upon simple request, and cannot be viewed by anyone other than you — myself included. However, public content you create via micou.org may be verified if reported. Private content (uploaded files) may be securely analyzed by low-energy algorithms that detect hate speech, incitement to violence against marginalized communities, or child pornography, without saving your data or human intervention. In case of substantiated report, your access may be revoked and your credentials transmitted to authorities according to the situation.`

**Rule 4**: `Everything on micou.org is, and will remain, fully encrypted, private and free. My goal is to enable individuals and collectives to access comprehensive tools to develop their projects, without depending on centralized, paid or for-profit providers. Some tools are created by me, others by open-source communities and adapted for micou.org.`

**Rule 5**: `Operating these tools has a cost. This is why I must process account creation requests to ensure reliability and accessibility of services. As the community grows, I will work on expanding the platform's capacity.`
